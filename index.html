Hi, I'm David Ma, an aspiring coder. Welcome to my Portfolio!

I was born in Beijing, China. My last name Ma (simplified Chinese: 马; traditional Chinese: 馬; pinyin: Mǎ) means horse in English, which is why the favicon of this portfolio site is a horse. When I was 6 I moved to Cleveland, Ohio, which is where I grew up. I am 2 years younger than Lebron James who is also from the Cleveland area, and was a big fan of Lebron until he left for the Miami Heat during the "Decision", but begrudgingly welcomed him back to Cleveland and was ultimately happy that the Cavs won their first championship.

I moved to NYC when I was 18 for college, where I studied Operations Research and Economics at Columbia. Since graduating I've worked in finance at the New York Stock Exchange, in a startup Openfolio, and currently in insurance at Marsh. I have a confession - I flunked my very first Intro to Computer Science class at Columbia, and my effort with this Bootcamp is to rectify that. I hope to use these new skills to create a website for my upcoming Children's Book, The Emperor's Seed. Most Importantly, I am hoping this class will serve as a foundation for learning coding in general including new languages, thought processes, and skills that can be useful for a lifetime.

Thank you for visiting. This is the first working portfolio page that I wrote from scratch. I hope you enjoyed your visit!
